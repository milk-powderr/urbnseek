  • Leg Warmers
  • Crotcheting / 2023
  • Wool yarn, crotcheting needle
  • Objective
  • To create a pair of leg warmers.
  • Process
  • I decided on a pastel theme with simple acessories. The body of the legwarmers are made using a half-double crotchet stitch and is lined with a scallop edge. There are bows that run down the outward sides of wach leg warmer.
