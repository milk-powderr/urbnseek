  • SLAP!
  • Packaging / 2023
  • Figma
  • Objective
  • To create a package for four beverage cans.
  • About
  • 'SLAP' is a brand of citrus-flavored energy drinks. This project in particualr is a 4-pack that contains fpur different flavors: lemon, keylime, mandarin and grapefruit.
  • Process
  • The name 'SLAP' is derived from the Nigerian slang 'slappy', which is used to describe sour food such as citrus fruit since the bite of sourness is sometimes akin to getting slapped in the face. The package has a tab lock top and a crash lock bottom. The images of fruit slices and plus-signs along with the logo have a pixellated look to them to attribute to the fact that this is an energy drink.
