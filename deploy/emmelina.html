  • Brand Identity / 2022
  • Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
  • Objective
  • To create a screenprint based on a podcast, displaying at least one instance of 'half-tone' in the design.
  • About
  • This screenprint is based on Episode 116: 'Something Blue' from the bi-weekly podcast 'Lore'. The podcast tells the story of how a woman scammed hundreds of wealthy people by making them believe that extreme fasting was the way to cure the body of 'all possible diseases'.
  • Process
  • I decided to use a picture of chewable vitamins. I edited them in Photoshop to reduce them to three 'layers'. The paper at the bottom was also edited to include a half-tone effect which was transferred to a silk screen along with the image of pills.
