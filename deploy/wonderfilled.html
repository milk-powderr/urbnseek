  • Wonderfilled
  • Motion Graphic / 2022
  • Figma, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GSAP, Visual Stusio Code, Codekit, GitHub
  • Objective
  • To code a short animation of a logo while following their brand style guides.
  • About
  • OREO is one of the most recognizable cookie brands in America if not the whole world. The OREO cookie comes in many flavours and sizes. Sometimes they're covered in chocolate, come in the form of an ice cream, and have even been adapted into drinks. At the end of the day, it all comes down to the original humble cookie: white cream sandwiched by two charcoal colored disks.
  • Process
  • Since OREO is an existing brand, it was necessary to follow their brand style guides: the font and color to use, how the cookie can be oriented, what wording is appropriate, and so on and so forth. This project began with a storyboard in Figma so there could be a proper roadmap for the animation. All assets of this project were made in Figma and the animation was created using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and GSAP. The phrases 'OREO COOKIES JUST WANNA DUNK' and 'Wonderfilled' were used to stay in line with the brand's playful and inviting tone.
